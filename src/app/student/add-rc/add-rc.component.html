PSYCHOMOTOR DOMAIN
Psychomotor

This refers to the skill of physical response to the situation the child is in. Psychomotor reflects the brain’s interaction with the environment. It can be categorized to gross motor development and fine motor development. The large body movements which involves the whole body, such as jumping, running, standing, balancing etc., are gross motor movements. Fine motor movements are the small body movements with fingers, wrist, hands, feet and toes.
